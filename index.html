Our Mission

The Faith Health Project is a Christian charity dedicated to providing vital support in areas of health, social care, and education, with a focus on empowering individuals to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives within their communities. We strive to uplift those in need through faith-based leadership, spiritual care, and practical support.

Who we help

We are committed to serving individuals and communities through a wide range of initiatives, including:.

Health & Social Support:

Providing essential information and advice on health and social issues, ensuring individuals are equipped with the knowledge to make informed decisions about their well-being.

Leadership Training:

Offering training and education to faith leaders, enabling them to better serve and support their communities with effectiveness and compassion.

Spiritual & Emotional Care

Offering pastoral care to those affected by illness, grief, or personal struggles, guiding them through these challenges with love and spiritual support.

Youth & Education:

Addressing the educational, health, and social needs of young people, helping them grow into responsible, empowered adults with bright futures.

HIV & Health Stigma:

Addressing the stigma around HIV and other health issues, fostering awareness, compassion, and understanding within communities to break down barriers and promote inclusive care.

Our Focus Areas

The Faith Health Project actively operates in key areas of need, including:

Offering pastoral support for bereaved or ill individuals.

Facilitating leadership training to bolster community effectiveness.

Tackling educational and health issues for vulnerable young people.

Working against stigma associated with HIV and other health concerns.
